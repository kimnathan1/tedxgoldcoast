RESILIENCE
IN PARADISE

From the mat to the boardroom.
Discover the true meaning of strength in Gold Coast.

The Theme

Paradise isn't just about relaxation.
It's about recovery.

Gold Coast is a world-renowned resort destination. But we look beyond the beauty of this place to the intense life and the process of Recovery that exists behind it.

Resilience in Paradise is not just about healing. We explore true 'resilience' through meditation to mend broken hearts, Jiu-Jitsu to test limits, and startup stories of rising from failure.
